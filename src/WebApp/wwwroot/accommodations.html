Accommodations

Business or Leisure

Whether you are traveling on business or leisure, the Tulip Inn offers a variety of modern room types including Queen or King Rooms, Rooms with a full Kitchen and Physically Challenged Rooms. All of our rooms are newly renovated and include work desks, free internet access and voice mail for the contemporary traveler. We offer you a comfortable night's rest at a reasonable price; and, in the morning to get you started we offer you complimentary continental breakfast to prepare you for the day.
